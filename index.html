Meet Your Instructor

Thomas is an American, Born in California but has been living in Vietnam for around 22 years. He holds a Red Cross Lifeguard Training Certification from the State University of New York (SUNY). He also holds an Advanced Open Water Diver (AOWD) SCUBA diving certification.

Participating in educational organizations, ocean maritime projects and travel industry positions has given him a close and personal understanding of the people and the landscape of the South East Asia. His experience with swimming and English training allows him to communicate well with curious youngsters, teenagers and adults alike
